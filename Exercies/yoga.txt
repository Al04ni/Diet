MORNING YOGA ROUTINE (ENERGIZING)  
-------------------------------------  
1️⃣Centering & Breathwork (3 min)  
   - Sit comfortably, close your eyes.  
   - Inhale deeply through your nose, exhale through your mouth.  
   - Repeat for 3 minutes, focusing on your breath.  

2️⃣Dynamic Warm-Up (5 min)  
   - Neck Rolls (30 sec)  
   - Shoulder Rolls (30 sec)  
   - Cat-Cow Pose (1 min)  
   - Spinal Twists (1 min)  
   - Downward Dog (1 min)  

3️⃣Main Flow (15–20 min)  
   - Sun Salutations (5 rounds)  
   - Warrior I & II (Hold each for 30 sec per side)  
   - Triangle Pose (Hold for 30 sec per side)  
   - Tree Pose (Balance for 30 sec per side)  
   - Cobra Pose (Hold for 30 sec)  
   - Child’s Pose (Rest for 1 min)  

4️⃣Cool-Down & Relaxation (5 min)  
   - Seated Forward Fold (Hold for 1 min)  
   - Supine Twist (30 sec per side)  
   - Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose (Hold for 2 min)  
   - Savasana (1 min)  

Pro Tip:Hydrate and practice gratitude before starting your day!  

=====================================  
NIGHT YOGA ROUTINE (RELAXING)  
-------------------------------------  
1️⃣Gentle Warm-Up (3–5 min) 
   - Seated Neck Stretches (30 sec)  
   - Shoulder Rolls (30 sec)  
   - Cat-Cow Pose (1 min)  
   - Forward Fold (1 min)  

2️⃣Slow & Deep Stretching (15 min)  
   - Child’s Pose (Hold for 2 min)  
   - Butterfly Pose (Hold for 2 min)  
   - Reclining Twist (Hold for 1 min per side)  
   - Seated Forward Fold (Hold for 2 min)  
   - Happy Baby Pose (Hold for 1 min)  

3️⃣Cool-Down & Meditation (5 min)  
   - Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose (Hold for 2 min)  
   - Deep belly breathing (1 min)  
   - Savasana (Final relaxation for 2 min)  

Pro Tip:Dim the lights, play soft music, and focus on deep breathing to improve sleep quality!
