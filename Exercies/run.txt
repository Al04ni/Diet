MORNING RUN ROUTINE (ENERGY BOOST)  
-------------------------------------  
1️⃣Warm-Up (5 min)  
   - Light jogging in place (1 min)  
   - High knees (30 sec)  
   - Arm swings & shoulder rolls (30 sec)  
   - Leg swings & ankle circles (1 min)  
   - Dynamic lunges (1 min)  
   - Deep breathing (1 min)  

2️⃣Main Run (20–30 min) 
   - Start with a slow jog for 3–5 min  
   - Increase pace to a comfortable run  
   - If experienced, add 5 min of sprint intervals  
   - Keep steady breathing  

3️⃣Cool-Down & Stretch (5–10 min) 
   - Slow jogging/walking (3 min)  
   - Standing hamstring stretch  
   - Quad stretch (hold each side for 20 sec)  
   - Hip flexor stretch  
   - Calf stretch against a wall  

Pro Tip:Hydrate before & after running!  
 
=====================================  

NIGHT RUN ROUTINE (WIND-DOWN)  
-------------------------------------  
1️⃣Warm-Up (5 min, Low Impact) 
   - Gentle walking (2 min)  
   - Arm circles & shoulder rolls (30 sec)  
   - Slow hip rotations (30 sec)  
   - Light quad & calf stretches  

2️⃣Main Run (15–25 min, Moderate Pace)  
   - Start with an easy jog for 3–5 min  
   - Keep a moderate, steady pace  
   - Avoid high-intensity sprints to prevent overstimulation before bed  

3️⃣Cool-Down & Relaxation (5–10 min) 
   - Walk for 3–5 min  
   - Deep breathing exercises (1 min)  
   - Static stretching (Hamstrings, Quads, Hips, Calves)  
   - Meditation or gentle yoga for full relaxation  

Pro Tip:Avoid caffeine before night runs & wear reflective gear!
